Alpha Research is a local command center for turning messy data and fuzzy research intent into durable research work: datasets, remote runs, analysis artifacts, and follow-up decisions.

Use it when you want leverage over data work, not when you want to operate infrastructure. Alpha Research should help you decide what data you have, what work is worth running, what happened in the run, and what to do next.

## Core concepts

- **Dataset**: a durable research environment, not just a single table.
- **Public canonical dataset**: a shared, evergreen dataset with stable id, source registry, normalized outputs, and provenance reports.
- **Private dataset**: a workspace-scoped upload or research environment visible only to the signed-in account.
- **Run**: a remote job that queries, transforms, labels, analyzes, or documents a dataset.
- **Artifact**: a saved result from a run, such as markdown, JSON, CSV, charts, transcripts, or dataset profiles.

The `research` CLI and dashboard work together across four jobs.
