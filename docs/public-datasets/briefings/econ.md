# Literal Data Inventory
- Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers: seasonally adjusted U.S. national monthly price-index observations; one row per month; United States national aggregate; January 1947 through March 2026; fields include observation date and CPI-U index value; unit is index level where 1982-1984 equals 100.
- Effective Federal Funds Rate: U.S. national monthly monetary-policy interest-rate observations; one row per month; United States; July 1954 through April 2026; fields include observation date and effective federal funds rate; unit is percent.
- 10-Year Treasury Constant Maturity Rate: U.S. daily Treasury yield observations for the 10-year maturity; one row per federal business day; United States Treasury securities; January 2, 1962 through May 6, 2026; fields include observation date and 10-year yield; unit is percent.
- Unemployment Rate: seasonally adjusted U.S. civilian labor-force unemployment observations; one row per month; United States national aggregate; January 1948 through April 2026; fields include observation date and unemployment rate; unit is percent.
- Gross Domestic Product: U.S. quarterly nominal output observations; one row per quarter; United States economy; first quarter 1947 through first quarter 2026; fields include observation date and GDP value; unit is billions of dollars.
- Federal Housing Finance Agency all-transactions house price index by state: state-quarter housing price index observations; one row per state and quarter; 50 states plus District of Columbia; first quarter 1975 through fourth quarter 2025; fields represent state, year, quarter, and not-seasonally-adjusted index value.
- Federal Housing Finance Agency all-transactions house price index by metropolitan area: metro-quarter housing price index observations; one row per metropolitan statistical area and quarter; U.S. metropolitan areas; first quarter 1975 through fourth quarter 2025; fields represent metro name, CBSA code, year, quarter, not-seasonally-adjusted index, and seasonally adjusted index.
- American Community Survey 2024 1-year Public Use Microdata Sample: person-level and housing-unit-level survey microdata; records represent people and housing units; geography is Public Use Microdata Areas across the United States and Puerto Rico; survey vintage is 2024; includes person records and housing records.
- Current Population Survey Basic Monthly January 2026 public-use sample: person-level and household-level labor-force survey microdata; records represent people and households; geography is U.S. national public-use sample geography; reference month is January 2026; includes public-use person and household records.
- American Housing Survey 2023 National Public Use File: housing-unit, mortgage, person, and project survey microdata; records represent housing units, mortgages, people, and housing projects; geography is U.S. national sample geography; survey vintage is 2023; includes household, mortgage, person, and project tables.
- Bureau of Economic Analysis state personal income data: annual state-level personal-income measures; records represent state-year regional income observations; geography covers U.S. states and the District of Columbia; coverage is 1969 through 2024; includes personal income, population, and related regional economic measures across multiple table files.
- U.S. Treasury Debt to the Penny: daily federal debt balance records; one row per record date; United States Treasury aggregate balances; most recent 5,000 daily records available in the stored extract; fields include record date, debt held by the public, intragovernmental holdings, and total public debt outstanding.
- Bank for International Settlements locational banking statistics: quarterly cross-border banking-position data; records represent reporting-country, counterparty-country, instrument, currency, sector, and quarter combinations; geography is global; coverage extends through fourth quarter 2025; fields describe cross-border claims, liabilities, positions, reporting areas, counterparties, instruments, sectors, currencies, and time periods.
- OECD Main Economic Indicators composite leading indicator for the United States: national composite leading-indicator observations; records represent United States indicator values by period; geography is United States; coverage extends through the current stored OECD release; fields include country, indicator, frequency, time period, and observed value.
- Zillow Home Value Index city series: city-month home-value index observations in wide time-series form; records represent U.S. cities with monthly home-value columns; geography covers U.S. cities and metros; coverage is January 2000 through March 2026; fields include region identifiers, city/metro/state descriptors, size rank, and monthly smoothed seasonally adjusted home-value index values for typical homes.
- National Bureau of Economic Research U.S. business cycle chronology: recession peak and trough chronology records; records represent U.S. business-cycle turning points; geography is United States national economy; coverage is 1854 through 2020; fields are encoded in an HTML table describing peak months, trough months, contraction durations, expansion durations, and cycle durations.
