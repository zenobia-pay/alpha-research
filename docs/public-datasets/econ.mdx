# Literal Data Inventory
- National macroeconomic time-series extracts from the Federal Reserve Economic Data portal: 940 monthly unemployment observations spanning January 1948 through April 2026, 951 monthly CPI readings from January 1947 through March 2026, 862 monthly effective federal funds rates from July 1954 through April 2026, 16,787 business-day 10-year Treasury yields from January 1962 through May 2026, and 317 quarterly nominal GDP values through first quarter 2026. Each table contains observation dates with a single national aggregate measure; no subnational breakdowns or alternate vintages are present.
- Federal Housing Finance Agency all-transactions house price index CSVs storing 10,403 quarterly state-level index records and 83,639 quarterly metro-level index records covering 1975Q1–2025Q4. Both panels provide index values per geography without county-level coverage or confidence intervals on disk.
- U.S. Treasury Fiscal Data extracts capturing 5,000 sequential Debt to the Penny daily balance records (1993-04-01–2013-03-12 in the current file because only the first page was saved) and 10,000 Daily Treasury Statement operating cash balance records (2014-06-04–2026-05-07) with account-type balances reported in millions of dollars. Historical pagination beyond those windows has not yet been retrieved.
- Bureau of Labor Statistics CPI-U all items JSON payload from the public API containing 120 monthly observations for 2015-01 through 2026-04 with year, period, value, and footnote fields for the national series only.
- Zillow Research city-level smoothed seasonally adjusted home value index matrix (license review) holding 21,410 location rows with 323 monthly columns spanning January 2000 through March 2026 for typical single-family home values. Redistribution requires honoring Zillow terms of use.
- Census Bureau microdata archives preserved as downloaded ZIP packages: the 2024 1-year American Community Survey PUMS person and housing files at PUMA geography, the January 2026 Current Population Survey basic monthly public-use file, and the 2023 American Housing Survey national public-use release with household, person, mortgage, and project tables. All remain in provider ZIP structure without in-volume extraction.
- Bureau of Economic Analysis CAINC1 regional personal income archive (ZIP) containing annual state-level income tables for 1969–2024 alongside layout documentation; files remain compressed pending extraction.
- Bank for International Settlements locational banking statistics bulk archive (license review) comprising quarterly cross-border position CSVs through 2025Q4 for reporting and counterparty countries. Legal review is required before redistribution and the archive is stored as delivered.
- OECD composite leading indicator SDMX JSON payload for the United States (license review) holding annual indicator values through the 2026 publication year with associated metadata; redistribution depends on OECD data terms.
- National Bureau of Economic Research business cycle chronology HTML snapshot (license review) preserving the table of U.S. recession peaks and troughs through 2020 for reference and further parsing.
